My story

I’m Lebogang Malefetse, a professional dancer and content creator from Johannesburg, South Africa. My journey started long before I ever stepped onto a stage or went viral online — it began in my living room, dancing to any song that played. What started as passion became purpose. I mix movement with fashion and storytelling to create content that connects.

From local shows to brand collaborations, I focus on energy, confidence and strong visuals. My dream is to represent South African dance on global stages and continue inspiring others to move boldly.
